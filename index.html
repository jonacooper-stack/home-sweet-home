Quick answers

Common questions.

What devices do you use?

Trusted brands your family probably already knows — Apple Watch, Amazon Echo, Ring, and a few smart-home sensors. Nothing weird, nothing medical-looking. We pick what fits your parent's needs and comfort level.

How fast can you get set up?

For urgent situations — like a parent coming home from the hospital — we can be in the house within 72 hours. For everyone else, usually a week.

What happens if my parent falls?

The devices we install can detect falls and contact emergency help directly. We make sure those features are turned on and that the right family members get notified, fast.

Why isn't the setup price listed?

Because every home is different. A small apartment needs a few devices. A larger home with more rooms, more entry points, or higher-end devices needs more. We give you an honest quote after a quick call — no pressure, no surprises.

What's the $89/month for?

Devices fail quietly. Batteries die. Watches end up on the dresser. The subscription is us catching all of that — checking the system every month, fixing what we can, and telling you in plain English what's working.

Will my mom feel like she's being watched?

No cameras unless she wants them — and she almost never does. She controls what's shared and with whom. The system fades into the background, the way it should.

We already own some devices. Do we start over?

Almost never. We use what you have, fill in the gaps, and only add what's actually needed.
